About Us

A Family-Owned NDIS Provider That Puts You First

Equal Care was founded with a simple mission: to provide care that is truly centred around each individual. We understand that no two people are the same, which is why we take a personalised approach to every participant's care.

Team

With a dedicated team of professionals who genuinely care about making a difference, we create a supportive, safe and empowering environment for every individual we work with.

 

All of our team is required to undergo a criminal history check (national police clearence) prior to being able to work at Equal Care.

Our Goal

Our focus is on enhancing quality of live, fostering independence, and making every participant feel valued and heard.

 

In addition, we also plan on expanding property options to provide the best living solutions for our participants.

Why Us

At Equal Care, we don't believe in 'one-size-fits-all' solutions. We work closely with you and your family to ensure that your care is tailored to your needs, preferences, and goals.

 

Offering highly trained support workers, a focus on independence, dignity, and well-being, personalised care plans and much more to come!

 

Join the Equal Care team today, and experience the difference of care that is truly equal, inclusive, and empowering.
