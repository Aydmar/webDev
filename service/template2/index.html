Equal Care Solutions

A family-owned and operated NDIS provider that puts it's clients/customers first! Your care, your way.

At Equal Care, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to support independence, well-being, and a high quality of live. Whether you need daily assistance, a comfortable home, or extra support in your journey, our dedicated team is here for you.

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

Safe, comfortable, and fully supported living spaces tailored to individual needs.

Specialised Disability Accomodation (SDA)

Helping participants access suitable SDA housing options.

Personalised In-Home Support

Assistance with daily activities, personal care, and household tasks.

Household Assistance & Cleaning

Maintaining a clean, safe, and organised living environment.

Transport & Community Access

Helping individuals stay connected with their community and attend appointments, events, and social activities.

Meal Preparation & Nutrition Support

Providing healthy meal options and assistance with cooking.

Live Skills & Capacity Building

Supporting independence through skill development in daily tasks, communication, and decision-making.

Equal Care Properties (Coming Soon)

We are expanding our services to include modern, accessible properties designed to offer safe, comfortable, and fully supported living options for NDIS participants. Stay tuned for updates!
